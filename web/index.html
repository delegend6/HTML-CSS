Types of trades we take

We take reversal trades and continuation trades at different levels.

Engulfing Pattern

The engulfing pattern is a reversal pattern in a prolong trending market. The engulfing pattern can either be traded at support as call option or at resistance as a put option.

Morning Star

The morning star is a call trading pattern which occours at the support of a long down trend. It comprises of three candlesticks forming a pattern.

Evening Star

The evening star is a put trade that occours at resistance level after a long uptrend. It is formed by 3 candle sticks.
